Santorini, Greece

Santorini is a picturesque island in the Aegean Sea known for its stunning sunsets, white-washed buildings perched on cliffs, and crystal-clear blue waters. This island's unique charm attracts honeymooners and travellers seeking a romantic gateway.

Mount Roraima, Venezuela

Mount Roraima is flat-topped mountain often shrouded in mist and clouds. It inspired Sir arthur Conan Doyle's "The Lost World" and is one of the oldest geological formations on Earth, making it a unique and mystical destination for trekkers.
